About

I'm a developer focused on building agent systems and multi-modal content pipelines — from ReAct loops and tool orchestration to video generation pipelines. Started the Agent journey in early 2026, transitioning from embedded systems engineering.

Currently transitioning from embedded systems engineering to AI Agent development, bringing years of experience in protocol stacks, system-level thinking, and resource-constrained optimization to the LLM era. I work with Python, TypeScript, and Go to build agents that use MCP protocol, RAG systems, and multi-model routing.

Previously, I worked on embedded systems at TP-Link (switch BSP, Ethernet RPC frameworks) and Jieli Technology (Bluetooth protocol stack, AI dialogue systems, embedded UI). These experiences shaped my approach to building systems that are both robust and efficient — qualities I now apply to Agent development.

Outside of coding, you can find me exploring the latest LLM research papers and building side projects with open-source AI tools.
